Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about booking, cancellation, and futsal facilities.

1.How do I book a court?

You can easily book a court by navigating to the Book page and filling out the booking form with your preferred date, time, and court type after login.

2.What is the cancellation policy?

Bookings can be canceled up to 24 hours in advance for a full refund. Cancellations made within 24 hours of the booking time may be subject to a cancellation fee.

3.Do you provide futsal equipment?

Yes, we offer futsal equipment for rent, including balls and bibs. You can add equipment rental to your booking at a nominal fee.
